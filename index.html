Why it exists

A test space for explaining complex ideas visually, quickly, and memorably.

AIVMeta explores how AI, math, and technical ideas can become easier to understand through short-form media. It is a creative lab for hooks, diagrams, generated visuals, voice, captions, and pacing—built to learn what helps people remember hard concepts without flattening them.
